The Jaipur Express Currency Folio

1701's currency folios are designed to carry up to four currencies in separate sections. Zippered and flap-magnet pockets provide storage space for your Dollars, Pounds, Reals, or Rupees, and the color distinctions help you remember where you kept what!

The Jaipur Express is inspired by the kitschy HORN OK PLEASE colours you see on Rajasthani journeys - next stop Jaipur.

A visual riot of threads, beads, mirrors, striking turquoise, a funky yellow back, and 1701's signature quilted suede interiors complete the look.

The body is made of fine Italian Turquoise Leather and Italian Lemon Leather. Entirely hand-embroidered thread, bead and mirror-work adorn the top and front flap.The interiors are lined with quilted suede and a silk paisley print fabric.
